ZenFlow - Account and Data Deletion

Last updated: May 11, 2026

ZenFlow currently stores productivity data locally on your device. You can delete your local account and associated app data directly inside the app.

Delete inside the app

  1. Open ZenFlow.
  2. Tap the profile button in the top-right corner.
  3. Go to Account.
  4. Tap Delete Account & Data.
  5. Confirm deletion.

What is deleted

  • Local profile details such as email and display name.
  • Tasks, habits, focus sessions, moods, goals, streaks, XP, settings, and profile image stored by ZenFlow on this device.
  • Local reminder schedules created by ZenFlow.

Need help

If you cannot access the app, contact us at zenflowapp142@gmail.com. Because ZenFlow data is stored locally on your device, uninstalling the app or clearing app storage from Android Settings also removes local app data.
