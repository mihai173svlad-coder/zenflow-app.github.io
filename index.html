🧘 ZenFlow — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

ZenFlow ("we", "our", or "the app") is a productivity application that helps users manage tasks, build habits, track focus sessions, and improve daily organization. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use ZenFlow:

  • Account Information: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google. We use this solely for authentication and syncing your data across devices.
  • User-Generated Data: Tasks, habits, focus sessions, settings, and other content you create within the app. This data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to Firebase Cloud Firestore if you sign in.
  • Profile Photo: If you choose to set a custom profile photo from your device's gallery, it is stored locally on your device only.
  • Purchase Information: Subscription and in-app purchase data is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To sync your data across devices (when signed in)
  • To send local notifications (reminders, streak alerts, focus session reminders)
  • To manage your premium subscription status

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: Your data is primarily stored on your device using AsyncStorage.
  • Cloud Storage: If you sign in, data is synced to Google Firebase Cloud Firestore, which uses industry-standard encryption.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

ZenFlow uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Cloud Firestore) — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing (for subscriptions/purchases) — Google Privacy Policy
  • Expo (development framework) — Expo Privacy Policy

5. Permissions

  • Notifications: To send reminders and alerts. You can disable these in your device settings.
  • Photo Library: Only accessed when you choose to set a profile picture. We do not access your photos without your explicit action.
  • Internet: Required for cloud sync, authentication, and subscription verification.

6. Children's Privacy

ZenFlow is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual tasks, habits, and data within the app
  • Signing out to remove cloud sync
  • Uninstalling the app to remove all local data
  • Contacting us at zenflowapp142@gmail.com to request full account and data deletion

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
